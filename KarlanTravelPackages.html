About Us

Although our deluxe package offers a more comprehensive experience, our light package covers all the essential Kjerag experience as well.

Flight

Our package includes a plane ticket from any part of the terra to Kjerag! Enjoy a confortable ride with great services.*

*Flight plans will be announced 12 hours before departure; no refunds and reschedule allowed. Oxygen may be provided. No food or drinks provided on board.

Accomodations

Enjoy our cozy hotel plan with free breakfast on site! Functional bathing and showering equipment provided.*

*Hot water is seasonal. Individual washroom is only available upon further requests.

Excursions

Do you love adventures? Then you will love our mountain sightseeing tour! You will be given the chance to fight for your own survival in the mountains of Kjerag. This self-guided tour would allow you to explore the wilderness without any guide rushing you.*

*By purchasing this package, you acknowledges the health risks of hiking, which includes but not limited to: strains,abdominal pain, allergic reactions, nausa, vomiting, diarrhea, frostbite, blisters, hypoxia, cardiac arrest.
